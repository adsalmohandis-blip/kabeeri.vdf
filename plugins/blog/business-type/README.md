# Business Types

`blog` fits creators and teams that publish regular articles, guides, or opinion content.

Common business types:
- Solo creator blog
- Multi-author editorial blog
- Technical blog
- Thought leadership site
- Product marketing blog
- Community knowledge blog

Typical goals:
- Publish articles on a steady cadence
- Support authors, drafts, and editorial review
- Improve SEO and discovery
- Capture newsletter or community signups

Key intake questions:
- Is this one author or many authors?
- Do posts need categories, tags, or series?
- Should the blog support comments or community discussion?
- Is the main goal education, authority building, or product marketing?

Module focus:
- Blog feed and article pages
- Author profiles
- Categories, tags, and search
- Draft and publish workflow
- Subscribe and share actions

Design tone:
- Content-first layout
- Readable typography
- Simple navigation and minimal distraction
