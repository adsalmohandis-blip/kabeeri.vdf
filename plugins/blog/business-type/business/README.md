# Business

Business blogs support marketing, lead capture, case studies, and product education.

Intake questions:
- Which business goals should the blog support?
- Do you need lead capture or conversion blocks?
- Should posts map to products, services, or campaigns?

Module map:
- content marketing home
- service or product CTA blocks
- case studies
- blog archive and SEO
- admin publishing

Sample acceptance criteria:
- The blog supports conversion-oriented content.
- Posts can link to business offers or campaigns.
- Search and category browsing are easy to use.

Starter task pack:
- define content pillars and CTA strategy
- build marketing blocks and case study pages
- add SEO-friendly archive and authoring
