# Local News

Local news sites serve communities with regional pages, alerts, and civic coverage.

Intake questions:
- What local regions or municipalities matter?
- Do you need weather, traffic, or emergency alerts?
- Should community pages or calendars be included?

Module map:
- regional home
- local sections and beats
- alerts and notices
- community pages
- newsroom admin

Sample acceptance criteria:
- Readers can find coverage by location.
- Alerts are visible when published.
- Community-specific content is easy to manage.

Starter task pack:
- define regions and local sections
- build local landing pages and alerts
- add community admin controls
