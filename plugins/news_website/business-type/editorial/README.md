# Editorial

Editorial news sites focus on breaking coverage, front-page curation, and fast publishing.

Intake questions:
- What beats or sections should the newsroom cover?
- Do you need live coverage or breaking updates?
- What editorial workflow and approval steps are required?

Module map:
- top stories homepage
- article and author pages
- section landing pages
- live updates or alerts
- editorial admin console

Sample acceptance criteria:
- Editors can publish and reorder top stories safely.
- Breaking updates are visible when needed.
- Authors and sections are easy to browse.

Starter task pack:
- define section structure and editorial flow
- build top stories and article pages
- add live coverage or alerts if needed
