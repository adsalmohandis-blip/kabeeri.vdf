# Business Types

`news-website` fits publishers that need fast updates, editorial structure, and audience growth.

Common business types:
- Local news outlet
- Editorial magazine
- Niche industry publication
- Newsletter-led media brand
- Independent newsroom
- Culture and lifestyle publication

Typical goals:
- Publish breaking news and scheduled stories
- Organize articles by section and topic
- Grow subscribers and repeat visitors
- Support editors, writers, and contributors

Key intake questions:
- Is the site breaking-news driven or evergreen content driven?
- Do you need live updates, newsletters, or paywalls?
- How many editorial roles and approval steps are required?
- What sections and categories must the site support?

Module focus:
- Homepage and latest stories
- Category and section pages
- Article pages and author pages
- Newsletter signup and subscription prompts
- Editor workflow and publishing controls

Design tone:
- High-contrast reading experience
- Strong content hierarchy
- Fast scanning for headlines and featured stories
