# Store

Core ecommerce storefront flows for catalog, cart, checkout, and order management.

Intake questions:
- Is this a single-brand store or a catalog-led storefront?
- What product types, variants, and filters are needed?
- Do you need shipping, pickup, or digital delivery?
- Should the site support discounts, tax, and guest checkout?

Module map:
- homepage and collections
- product catalog and filters
- product detail and variants
- cart and checkout
- order history and account
- admin catalog management

Sample acceptance criteria:
- Customers can browse, filter, and view products.
- Cart and checkout preserve quantities and variants.
- Orders are recorded with payment and fulfillment state.
- Admin can manage products and pricing.

Starter task pack:
- define catalog structure and product variants
- build browsing and product detail flow
- add cart, checkout, and order history
- create admin product management
