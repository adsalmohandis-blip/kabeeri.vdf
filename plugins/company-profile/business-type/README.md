# Business Types

`company-profile` fits organizations that need a credible public presence and a clear service story.

Common business types:
- Corporate brand site
- Consulting firm
- Agency portfolio
- SaaS company profile
- Professional services firm
- Startup landing site

Typical goals:
- Introduce the company and its value proposition
- Show services, industries, and proof points
- Capture leads with a contact or inquiry form
- Build trust with case studies, testimonials, and team profiles

Key intake questions:
- What does the company sell?
- Is the site focused on brand trust, lead generation, or both?
- Do you need locations, team pages, or case studies?
- Should the site support multiple languages or regions?

Module focus:
- Hero and company story
- Services and capabilities
- Case studies and testimonials
- Team and careers
- Contact and inquiry capture

Design tone:
- Clean, polished, and credible
- Professional typography
- Strong hierarchy for services and proof
