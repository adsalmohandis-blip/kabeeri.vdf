# Agency

Agency profiles focus on service credibility, portfolio proof, and lead conversion.

Intake questions:
- Which services are your strongest offer?
- What case studies or portfolio items should be highlighted?
- What conversion path should the site drive?

Module map:
- services and packages
- portfolio and case studies
- testimonials and proof
- inquiry or booking CTA
- team and capabilities

Sample acceptance criteria:
- A visitor can understand services and capabilities quickly.
- At least one case study or proof block is present.
- The main inquiry path is visible on every key page.

Starter task pack:
- define services and lead funnel
- build portfolio and proof sections
- add conversion-oriented contact flow
