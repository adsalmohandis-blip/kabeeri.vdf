# Service

Service POS packs are for appointments, deposits, invoices, and service tickets.

Intake questions:
- Do you need deposits or invoicing?
- Should service tickets link to appointments?
- What payment capture rules are required?

Module map:
- service ticket
- appointment checkout
- deposit handling
- invoice and receipt
- reporting

Sample acceptance criteria:
- Service tickets can be checked out correctly.
- Deposits and invoices are supported.
- Staff can review service history.

Starter task pack:
- define service ticket and billing rules
- build checkout and invoice screens
- add deposit and reporting flows
