# Business Types

`pos` fits point-of-sale and transaction-heavy operations that need fast checkout and inventory awareness.

Common business types:
- Retail store POS
- Restaurant POS
- Cafe or quick-service POS
- Salon or spa POS
- Clinic or appointment checkout
- Pop-up or event sales station

Typical goals:
- Process sales quickly at the counter
- Track products, services, and inventory
- Handle receipts, discounts, and taxes
- Support staff roles and shifts

Key intake questions:
- Is this retail, food service, or service-based checkout?
- Do you need inventory, table management, or appointment checkout?
- What hardware integrations are required?
- How many cashier or manager roles are needed?

Module focus:
- Checkout and payment flow
- Products, services, and modifiers
- Inventory and stock alerts
- Receipts, refunds, and discounts
- Shift, staff, and register management

Design tone:
- Very fast and touch-friendly
- High contrast and low friction
- Clear states for payment and completion
