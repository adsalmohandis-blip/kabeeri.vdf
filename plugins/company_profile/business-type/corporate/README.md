# Corporate

Corporate profiles are for larger organizations that need governance, business-unit clarity, and a polished public presence.

Intake questions:
- What divisions, regions, or brands must be represented?
- Do you need governance, investor, or careers pages?
- What approval flow is required for content updates?

Module map:
- company overview
- divisions and regions
- leadership and governance
- careers and investor pages
- contact and legal footer

Sample acceptance criteria:
- The site supports multiple business units or regions.
- Leadership and governance content is easy to find.
- Publishing is safe for approved corporate updates.

Starter task pack:
- map divisions and navigation structure
- build governance and leadership pages
- add careers and investor entry points
