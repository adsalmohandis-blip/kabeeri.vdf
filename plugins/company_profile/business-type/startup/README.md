# Startup

Startup profile sites are for fast-moving companies that need a clear pitch, credibility, and hiring or investor signals.

Intake questions:
- What problem does the startup solve?
- Do you need investor, hiring, or lead-generation focus?
- What proof points matter most right now?

Module map:
- hero and value proposition
- founder story and mission
- product or service overview
- investor or hiring section
- contact and conversion CTA

Sample acceptance criteria:
- The site states the startup's value proposition within the first screen.
- At least one trust or proof section is visible.
- The primary CTA is obvious and reachable from the home page.

Starter task pack:
- define the startup narrative and CTA
- build hero, about, and proof sections
- add investor or hiring route if needed
