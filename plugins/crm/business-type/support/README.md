# Support

Support CRM packs are for tickets, cases, SLAs, and customer service operations.

Intake questions:
- Do you need tickets, cases, or both?
- What SLA rules and escalation paths apply?
- Should customer history be front and center?

Module map:
- ticket intake
- case detail
- SLA tracking
- customer timeline
- support reporting

Sample acceptance criteria:
- Support staff can see ticket status and SLA info.
- Customer history is attached to each case.
- Escalation rules are visible.

Starter task pack:
- define ticket and SLA flow
- build case detail and queue views
- add customer history and escalation rules
