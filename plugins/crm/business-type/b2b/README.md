# B2B

B2B CRM packs are for account hierarchies, proposals, and complex relationship management.

Intake questions:
- Do you need account hierarchies or parent-child relationships?
- Should quoting or proposals be included?
- What team collaboration rules matter most?

Module map:
- accounts and contacts
- opportunity tracking
- quotes and proposals
- relationship map
- reporting and collaboration

Sample acceptance criteria:
- Account structures are easy to model.
- Quotes or proposals are trackable.
- Multiple users can collaborate on the same account.

Starter task pack:
- define account hierarchy rules
- build opportunities and quotes
- add collaboration and reporting views
