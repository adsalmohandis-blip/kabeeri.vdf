# Business Types

`crm` fits teams that need customer tracking, sales workflows, or service follow-up.

Common business types:
- Sales-led CRM
- Support desk CRM
- Account management CRM
- Field service CRM
- Agency client tracking
- Internal customer operations platform

Typical goals:
- Track leads, contacts, and accounts
- Manage pipelines, tasks, and follow-ups
- Support service tickets or cases
- Surface team performance and reminders

Key intake questions:
- Is the primary goal sales, support, or account management?
- Do users need pipelines, cases, or both?
- Should the CRM integrate with email, chat, or phone logging?
- Which roles need dashboards and access control?

Module focus:
- Contacts and accounts
- Leads and pipeline stages
- Tasks, notes, and reminders
- Tickets, cases, or service requests
- Team dashboards and reporting

Design tone:
- Efficient and data-dense
- Clear filters and status badges
- Fast access to next action
