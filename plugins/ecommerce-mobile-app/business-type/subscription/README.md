# Subscription

Subscription apps focus on recurring billing, memberships, and account entitlements.

Intake questions:
- What subscription plans or tiers are needed?
- Do you need renewal, pause, or upgrade flows?
- Should member-only content be exposed in the app?

Module map:
- plan browsing
- subscribe and renew
- member account
- entitlement access
- billing notifications

Sample acceptance criteria:
- Users can subscribe and manage renewals.
- Account screens show plan status clearly.
- Member access is visible after login.

Starter task pack:
- define plans and renewal rules
- build subscribe and account screens
- add billing and entitlement notifications
