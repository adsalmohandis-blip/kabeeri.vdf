# Business Types

`ecommerce-mobile-app` fits commerce brands that need a mobile-first shopping experience.

Common business types:
- Retail store app
- Direct-to-consumer brand
- Marketplace app
- Subscription commerce app
- Local commerce or delivery app
- Luxury or boutique commerce app

Typical goals:
- Browse products quickly on mobile
- Support cart, checkout, and account flows
- Show order status, promotions, and loyalty features
- Encourage repeat purchases and push notifications

Key intake questions:
- Is this a catalog app, a marketplace, or a single-brand store?
- What payment and fulfillment methods are required?
- Do users need wishlists, subscriptions, or loyalty rewards?
- Should the app support guest checkout and saved addresses?

Module focus:
- Home and product discovery
- Product detail and variants
- Cart and checkout
- Account, orders, and notifications
- Promotions and loyalty

Design tone:
- Fast, thumb-friendly interaction
- Strong product imagery
- Minimal friction in checkout
