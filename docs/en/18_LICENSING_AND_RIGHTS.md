# 18 - Licensing and Rights

Licensing and rights must be clear before public distribution.

## Rules

- Keep the repository license visible.
- Do not commit client-private assets or secrets.
- Do not copy third-party designs, code, images, or docs without rights.
- Keep generated AI output reviewable and attributable to the project workflow.
- Treat commercial client work separately from reusable framework code.
