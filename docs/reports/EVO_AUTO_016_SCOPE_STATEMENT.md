# EVO_AUTO_016 Scope Statement

## Priority

- ID: `evo-auto-016`
- Title: `Historical folder/version clarity`
- Source: `technical_debt_review`

## Scope

This priority keeps historical folders, archived reports, and older planning material clearly labeled so runtime and future sessions do not treat them as current product truth.

The scope includes:

- historical labeling for archived reports and planning files
- a clear boundary between live state and historical evidence
- source-truth validation for historical surfaces
- documentation that explains what is historical and what is current

## Out of scope

- deleting archive history
- rewriting live runtime state as historical
- collapsing live and archived folders into one surface
- changing unrelated capability behavior

## Success criteria

- historical validation passes
- archived planning is visibly historical in docs and reports
- live state remains distinct from archive evidence
- future sessions can resume without confusing archive with current truth
