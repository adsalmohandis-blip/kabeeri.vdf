# EVO_AUTO_039 Scope Statement

## Priority

- ID: `evo-auto-039-blocked-scenarios`
- Title: `Blocked Scenario Reporting`
- Source: `new_features_docs_study`

## Scope

This priority reports blocked or invalid scenarios clearly so the developer knows what cannot proceed and why.

The scope includes:

- blocked-scenarios report output
- blocker and warning visibility
- readiness/governance references

## Out of scope

- treating blocked states as ready states
- removing the readiness or governance connection
- hiding the reason a scenario is blocked

## Success criteria

- the blocked report remains readable
- the blocker count remains visible
- the report still points to actionable next steps
