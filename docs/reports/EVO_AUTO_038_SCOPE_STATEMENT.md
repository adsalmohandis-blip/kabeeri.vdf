# EVO_AUTO_038 Scope Statement

## Priority

- ID: `evo-auto-038-full-task-coverage`
- Title: `Full Task Coverage Workflow`
- Source: `new_features_docs_study`

## Scope

This priority ensures each task is broken into complete execution slices with no remainder outside the queue.

The scope includes:

- task coverage board
- empty-board behavior
- full coverage policy

## Out of scope

- allowing partially covered tasks to stay visible without a full slice
- hiding the empty-board case
- replacing the coverage board with ad hoc notes

## Success criteria

- the coverage board remains valid
- the coverage policy stays explicit
- tasks are not left partially represented
