# General AI Coding Rules

- Work from the current Kabeeri task, not from a broad product wish.
- Do not invent requirements that are not in the task, context pack, ADR, or approved design/spec.
- Prefer the repository's existing framework, style, helpers, and tests.
- Keep changes small enough to review.
- Do not write secrets, credentials, or private customer data.
- Call out uncertainty instead of hiding it in code.
