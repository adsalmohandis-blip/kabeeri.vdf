# Checkout Flow

Use for e-commerce, marketplace, delivery, subscriptions, and paid booking flows.

Steps:

1. Review cart or selected item.
2. Capture contact, address, delivery, or service details.
3. Show shipping, taxes, fees, discounts, and total clearly.
4. Confirm payment or order placement.
5. Show processing, success/failure, and receipt or next steps.

Required states:

- Cart empty
- Invalid details
- Payment processing
- Payment failed
- Order success

Rules:

- Price and fees must not be hidden.
- Primary purchase action should be visually clear.
- Provide recovery paths for failed payment or invalid address.
