# Content And Microcopy Review Checklist

## Actions

- Primary actions describe outcomes.
- Destructive actions name the object or consequence.
- Button verbs are consistent across the screen.
- Icon-only actions have accessible names.

## States

- Empty states explain what is missing and the next action.
- No-results states preserve filters and offer reset.
- Error states explain recovery.
- Loading and progress copy is honest and useful.
- Success messages state what changed.

## Forms

- Labels are visible and specific.
- Helper text explains format, limits, or consequence.
- Validation messages are actionable.
- Sensitive flows avoid leaking private or technical data.

## Product Fit

- Tone matches business type and risk.
- Dashboards use operational language, not marketing copy.
- Commerce flows show price, fee, stock, delivery, and payment clarity.
- AI flows explain generation state, limitations, retry, and edit paths.
- Content pages use readable headings, summaries, dates, and source labels.

## Localization

- Arabic or bilingual copy is natural and direction-aware.
- Numbers, currency, dates, and units are unambiguous.
- Text fits containers in supported languages.
