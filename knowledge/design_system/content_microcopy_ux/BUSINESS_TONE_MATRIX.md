# Business Tone Matrix

## Admin / ERP / CRM

- Tone: precise, calm, direct.
- Avoid: marketing phrases, jokes, vague success messages.
- Use: exact record names, dates, status labels, permission language.

## eCommerce / Marketplace

- Tone: helpful, transparent, confidence-building.
- Avoid: hidden fees, vague delivery promises, pressure copy.
- Use: price, stock, shipping, return, trust, and checkout clarity.

## SaaS / AI Product

- Tone: efficient, guided, transparent.
- Avoid: overpromising automation or certainty.
- Use: setup progress, usage limits, saved state, retry/edit paths.

## HealthTech / FinTech

- Tone: precise, careful, reassuring.
- Avoid: casual language around risk, money, or health.
- Use: clear consent, permission, privacy, amount, date, and audit copy.

## Corporate / Landing / Content

- Tone: readable, credible, outcome-oriented.
- Avoid: generic hype and vague slogans.
- Use: direct value, proof, source labels, contact clarity.

## Arabic / Bilingual

- Tone should be natural in Arabic, not literal English.
- Use clear action verbs.
- Avoid mixed direction ambiguity in numbers, currencies, and dates.
- Keep Arabic UI copy shorter where containers are compact.
