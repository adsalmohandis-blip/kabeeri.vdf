# CORP-UI01 Corporate Home

## Identity

- Design code: `CORP-UI01`
- Business: corporate
- View: company homepage
- Style: professional credibility homepage

## Layout Anatomy

```text
nav
hero
service overview
proof/case strip
about signal
contact CTA
```

## UX Goals

- Explain what the company does.
- Establish credibility.
- Route visitors to services or contact.

## Required Components

- `CorporateHero`
- `ServiceOverview`
- `ProofStrip`
- `CaseTeaser`
- `ContactCTA`

## Required States

- default;
- contact CTA loading;
- missing case proof fallback.

## Data Requirements

- company positioning;
- services;
- proof points;
- contact route.

## Accessibility

- Semantic sections and headings.
- Keyboard-accessible navigation.
- Contact CTA labels are specific.

## Motion

- `EXPRESSIVE_MOTION`
- Section reveal is allowed.
- Avoid decorative intro delays.

## Common Mistakes

- Generic “we help businesses grow” messaging.
- Tiny contact path.
- Abstract visuals with no company signal.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Visitor can identify industry and offer immediately.
- Contact or service path is clear.

## Task Seed

- Build corporate home with services, proof, case teaser, and contact CTA.
