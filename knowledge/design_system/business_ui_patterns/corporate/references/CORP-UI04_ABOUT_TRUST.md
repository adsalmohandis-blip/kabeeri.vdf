# CORP-UI04 About Trust

## Identity

- Design code: `CORP-UI04`
- Business: corporate
- View: about, team, values, credibility
- Style: human trust page

## Layout Anatomy

```text
mission
team or leadership
timeline
values
credentials
CTA
```

## UX Goals

- Build human and institutional trust.
- Explain why the company exists.
- Reinforce credibility.

## Required Components

- `MissionBlock`
- `TeamGrid`
- `Timeline`
- `ValuesList`
- `CredentialBadge`
- `CTAButton`

## Required States

- no team photos;
- missing credentials;
- responsive team grid.

## Data Requirements

- mission;
- team members;
- milestones;
- values;
- credentials.

## Accessibility

- Team images include alt text.
- Timeline is readable without visual line.
- Credentials use text labels.

## Motion

- `EXPRESSIVE_MOTION`
- Timeline reveal is allowed.
- Avoid motion that makes people or credentials hard to inspect.

## Common Mistakes

- Vague mission statements.
- Team section with no context.
- Values as buzzwords only.

## Acceptance Criteria

- About page increases trust and provides next contact path.

## Task Seed

- Build about/trust page with mission, team, timeline, values, credentials, and CTA.
