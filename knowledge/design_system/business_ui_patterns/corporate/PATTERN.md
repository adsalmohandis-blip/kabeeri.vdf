# Corporate Website UI Pattern Pack

Use this pack for company websites, agencies, service providers, consultancies, B2B firms, and professional organizations.

## Design Posture

- Priority: trust, capability clarity, proof, and contact routing.
- Density: comfortable.
- Motion: `EXPRESSIVE_MOTION` but controlled.

## Required Views

- Corporate home
- Services
- Case studies
- About
- Contact

## Required Flows

- `ONBOARDING_FLOW.md` only when lead qualification or guided inquiry exists

## Creative Axes

- Brand authority level
- Service navigation depth
- Case proof emphasis
- Contact form complexity
- Motion restraint

## Acceptance Criteria

- Services are understandable.
- Contact path is visible.
- Proof supports claims.
- Corporate tone matches domain risk.
