# SaaS UI Pattern Pack

Use this pack for subscription products, workspaces, team tools, project systems, billing settings, and product dashboards.

## Design Posture

- Priority: activation, clarity, habit formation, account confidence.
- Density: balanced.
- Motion: `BALANCED_MOTION`.
- Dashboard reference: `ADMIT-ADB05` for clean modern SaaS surfaces.

## Required Views

- Product dashboard
- Onboarding checklist
- Settings and billing
- Usage or activity
- Empty first-run screens

## Required Flows

- `ONBOARDING_FLOW.md`
- `CRUD_FLOW.md`
- `USER_MANAGEMENT_FLOW.md`

## Creative Axes

- Empty-state tone
- Workspace navigation
- Billing surface style
- Setup checklist shape
- Helpful microcopy intensity

## Acceptance Criteria

- First-run empty states guide users to activation.
- Settings are grouped by account, workspace, billing, team, and security.
- Billing and usage states are explicit.
- Success feedback exists after setup changes.
