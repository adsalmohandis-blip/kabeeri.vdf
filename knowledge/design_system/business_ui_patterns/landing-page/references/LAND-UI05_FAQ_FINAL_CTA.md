# LAND-UI05 FAQ Final CTA

## Identity

- Design code: `LAND-UI05`
- Business: landing-page
- View: FAQ and final conversion
- Style: objection handling and clean closing action

## Layout Anatomy

```text
FAQ list
objection answers
support link
final CTA
secondary contact path
```

## UX Goals

- Answer final objections.
- Reduce anxiety.
- Provide a final conversion opportunity.

## Required Components

- `FAQAccordion`
- `SupportLink`
- `FinalCTA`
- `SecondaryContact`

## Required States

- expanded question;
- collapsed question;
- no FAQ;
- CTA loading.

## Data Requirements

- questions;
- answers;
- support contact;
- CTA target.

## Accessibility

- Accordion uses proper buttons.
- Focus states are visible.
- Answers remain readable on mobile.

## Motion

- `BALANCED_MOTION`
- Accordion open/close can transition.
- Do not hide important answer text behind long animation.

## Common Mistakes

- FAQ that repeats marketing copy.
- Final CTA with different promise than hero.
- No support path for remaining objections.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Final CTA is consistent with hero.
- FAQ is keyboard accessible.

## Task Seed

- Build FAQ and final CTA with accessible accordion, support path, and consistent conversion action.
