# Landing Page UI Pattern Pack

Use this pack for single-offer pages, launch pages, campaign pages, waitlists, and conversion-focused public pages.

## Design Posture

- Priority: first-viewport clarity, proof, conversion, fast scanning.
- Density: comfortable, with focused sections.
- Motion: `EXPRESSIVE_MOTION` when it supports storytelling.
- Dashboard reference: none by default.

## Required Views

- Hero and offer
- Benefits and feature proof
- Social proof
- Pricing or signup
- FAQ and final CTA

## Required Flows

- `ONBOARDING_FLOW.md` when signup or waitlist exists

## Creative Axes

- Hero media treatment
- CTA rhythm
- Proof placement
- Section pacing
- Motion expressiveness

## Acceptance Criteria

- The offer is clear in the first viewport.
- Primary CTA is visible and repeated at natural decision points.
- Proof appears before high-commitment forms.
- Page remains fast and accessible.
