# E-commerce UI Pattern Pack

Use this pack for stores, catalog pages, product discovery, cart, checkout, loyalty, and merchant admin surfaces.

## Design Posture

- Priority: discovery, trust, price clarity, low-friction purchase.
- Density: balanced on storefront, compact in admin order/product screens.
- Motion: `BALANCED_MOTION`.
- Dashboard reference: `ADMIT-ADB02` for commerce metrics and operational cards.

## Required Views

- Category and search results
- Product card and product details
- Cart or cart drawer
- Checkout
- Order confirmation
- Account and order tracking

## Required Flows

- `CHECKOUT_FLOW.md`
- `PAYMENT_CONFIRMATION_FLOW.md`
- `CRUD_FLOW.md` for admin catalog screens

## Creative Axes

- Product card style
- Trust signal placement
- Checkout rhythm
- Image ratio and gallery treatment
- Promotional intensity

## Acceptance Criteria

- Price, stock, shipping, and primary purchase action are visible.
- Add-to-cart, wishlist, view, remove, and checkout actions use mapped icons.
- Cart, checkout, and payment states include loading, error, success, and disabled states.
- Product lists include empty and filter-no-results states.
