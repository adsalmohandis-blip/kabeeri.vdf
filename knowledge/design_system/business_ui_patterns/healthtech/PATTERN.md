# HealthTech UI Pattern Pack

Use this pack for patient portals, appointments, medical records, prescriptions, lab results, and clinician dashboards.

## Design Posture

- Priority: clarity, calm tone, readability, safety, accessibility.
- Density: comfortable for patients, compact for clinicians.
- Motion: `MINIMAL_MOTION`.

## Required Views

- Patient profile
- Appointments
- Medical records
- Prescriptions
- Lab results / clinician dashboard

## Required Flows

- `BOOKING_FLOW.md`
- `USER_MANAGEMENT_FLOW.md`

## Creative Axes

- Clinical calmness
- Reading comfort
- Critical alert treatment
- Appointment flow clarity
- Senior-friendly spacing

## Acceptance Criteria

- Critical information is prominent and calm.
- Medical data is readable and not hidden.
- Accessibility is treated as core, not optional.
