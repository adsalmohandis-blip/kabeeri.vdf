# Booking UI Pattern Pack

Use this pack for appointments, reservations, services, rentals, and scheduling products.

## Design Posture

- Priority: availability clarity, confidence, summary before commitment.
- Density: balanced.
- Motion: `BALANCED_MOTION`.
- Dashboard reference: `ADMIT-ADB05` for clean service booking and summary cards.

## Required Views

- Service selection
- Calendar and time slots
- User details
- Booking summary
- Confirmation and reschedule

## Required Flows

- `BOOKING_FLOW.md`
- `PAYMENT_CONFIRMATION_FLOW.md` when paid

## Creative Axes

- Calendar density
- Slot card style
- Summary placement
- Policy visibility
- Confirmation tone

## Acceptance Criteria

- Available, unavailable, selected, and recently unavailable slots are visually distinct.
- Booking summary appears before confirmation.
- Cancellation policy is visible.
- Reschedule and cancellation states are supported.
