# Brand Identity Template

## Brand Name

Project or product name.

## Brand Personality

Plain words describing tone and visual feel.

## Audience

Who the interface is for.

## Logo And Marks

Approved logo files, usage rules, and forbidden usage.

## Colors

Approved colors and semantic usage.

## Typography

Approved font family, scale, and fallback.

## Imagery

Allowed image style, icon style, illustration style, and asset sources.

## Accessibility Constraints

Minimum contrast, RTL/LTR support, readable sizing, and reduced motion needs.

## Approval

Owner/client/designer approval.
